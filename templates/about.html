About

No availability at the campground you'd like to visit? Select the sites you'd like to visit, add a check in and check out date, and you'll receive a text message when there is availability. Because getting outside shouldn't be competitive or require months in advance of planning.

If you live in California, you know how bad booking a campground can be. The one time I got a campsite at Marin's Steep Ravine, I spent days refreshing the availability page on the hour. My hope is for this site to solve this.
